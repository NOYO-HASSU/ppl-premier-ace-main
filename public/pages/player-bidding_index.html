Player Bidding Process

Understand the rules and steps for drafting players in the Premier Padel League

Rewards and Incentives

All selected players receive a monthly engagement allowance and additional travel allowance.

Player Registration

  • Open to all players (minimum age 16 years)
  • Register through official form with CNIC, contact, and short video
  • Registration Fee: PKR 5,000 (non-refundable)

Final player list (with base prices) will be shared with all clubs before the auction.

🎯

Live Player Bidding

  • Conducted as a live auction (venue will be disclosed later)
  • The highest bidder wins after 10 seconds of no new bids
  • The player signs with the winning club immediately
📜

Contracts & Payments

  • 1-season contract supervised by PPL
  • Payment: 50% before first match, 50% after league end
  • Unsold players move to Reserve Pool
🔍

Transparency

  • Entire auction recorded or live-streamed
  • No private signings allowed without PPL approval
  • All franchises must follow the league's code of conduct

Key Dates

  • Player Registration: 1 November 2025 to 30 November 2025
  • Club Bidding: 3 December to 5 December 2025
  • Trials: 7 December to 13 December 2025
  • Category Finalization: 16 December 2025
  • Player Bidding: 17 to 19 December 2025
  • League Start: 1st January 2026
