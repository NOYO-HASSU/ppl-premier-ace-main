How to Register for PPL

Everything you need to know to become a part of the Premier Padel League

💰

Rewards and Incentives

All selected players receive a monthly engagement allowance and additional travel allowance.

👤

Who Can Register

  • Open to players aged 16 or above
  • Must be based in Pakistan
  • Requires basic padel or tennis experience
📝

How to Register

Option 1 – Online: Fill the official form at Player Registration Form

Option 2 – Offline: Submit form at PPL registration desks located at approved Padel Clubs/Arenas

ℹ️

Information Required

  • Full Name, CNIC/B-Form
  • Contact Number (WhatsApp)
  • Playing Experience (Beginner/Intermediate/Professional)
  • Preferred Side (Right/Left)
  • Optional: 1–2 minute playing video
💸

Registration Fee

PKR 5,000 (non-refundable)

Payment via Bank Transfer

  • Account Title: PREMIER PADEL LEAGUE (PVT) LTD
  • Bank Name: Meezan Bank
  • Account Number: 08350113629848

Attach screenshot or receipt with your form.

✅

Confirmation & Trials

After submission, you'll receive a confirmation SMS/WhatsApp with:

  • Registration ID
  • Trial venue, date & time

Players will attend official trials where PPL management evaluates skills and assigns categories: Alpha | Bravo | Charlie

🎯

Auction & Selection

  • Categorized players are added to the Official Player Bidding Catalogue
  • During the Live Auction, clubs bid for players
  • Selected players receive official contract & payment details from PPL

Key Notes

  • Registration fee is non-refundable
  • Players must appear for trials on time
  • Fake or incomplete information = disqualification
