Privacy Policy

Premier Padel League Pakistan (PPL)

Last Updated: 21/1/2026

Premier Padel League Pakistan ("PPL", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of users who access our website, mobile application, events, and related services.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with PPL.

1. SCOPE OF THIS POLICY

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The official PPL website
  • The official PPL mobile application
  • Player registrations, club registrations, franchise registrations
  • Event participation, auctions, and league-related activities
  • Communications with PPL via email, phone, or digital platforms

For mobile application–specific practices, this policy should be read together with our App Privacy Policy available at: https://privacypolicy.premierpadelleague.pk/

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • CNIC / ID details (where required for verification)
  • Date of birth
  • City and location
  • Player, club, or franchise details

b) Technical & Usage Information

  • IP address
  • Device and browser type
  • Pages visited and interactions
  • App usage data and performance logs

c) Transactional Information

  • Registration records
  • Auction participation data
  • Payment confirmations (processed through secure third-party gateways)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your information may be used to:

  • Register and manage players, clubs, franchises, and partners
  • Operate league competitions and auctions
  • Communicate important league updates and announcements
  • Improve our website, app, and services
  • Ensure compliance with league rules and legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud, misuse, or unauthorized activity

4. DATA SHARING & DISCLOSURE

PPL does not sell or rent your personal data.

Information may only be shared:

  • With trusted service providers (IT, hosting, payment processing)
  • With league officials and governing bodies where necessary
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process

All third parties are required to maintain confidentiality and data security.

5. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including:

  • Secure servers
  • Restricted access to sensitive information
  • Encrypted communication where applicable

While we strive to protect your data, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill league operations
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

7. USER RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

8. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our website or app may contain links to third-party websites or platforms. PPL is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third parties.

9. NO AFFILIATION / NO IMPERSONATION DISCLAIMER

Premier Padel League Pakistan (PPL Pakistan) is an independent sports organization.

PPL Pakistan:

  • Is not affiliated, associated, endorsed, or connected with any other league, organization, or entity using similar names or abbreviations
  • Does not impersonate any international, regional, or foreign padel league
  • Operates solely under its own management, branding, governance, and legal structure within Pakistan

Any similarity in name or abbreviation is coincidental and does not imply association.

10. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
