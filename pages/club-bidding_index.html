Eligibility Criteria for Clubs

To qualify for bidding, each club or owner must meet the following requirements:

  • 1. Financial Stability
    • Proof of financial capacity to manage club operations and player contracts.
    • Ability to pay the registration and bidding fees on time.
  • 2. Organizational Credibility
    • Must be a registered business entity or sports management group.
    • Clean record no involvement in fraud, litigation, or sports misconduct.
  • 3. Sports Involvement (Preferred)
    • Prior experience in managing or sponsoring sports teams (padel).
    • Commitment to developing local padel talent.
  • 4. Facilities & Infrastructure (Optional Bonus)
    • Should own or be able to collaborate with already present paddle clubs.
  • 5. Commitment to League Vision
    • Willingness to follow league rules, marketing guidelines, and fair play policies.
    • Active participation in promotional events and fan engagement activities.
    • Will abide by the PPL sponsorship guidelines.
