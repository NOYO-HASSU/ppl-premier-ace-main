Rules & Instructions

Complete guide to bidding, registration and player recruitment process

Club Eligibility

Clubs must be registered business entities with financial stability and commitment to PPL values. Each club enters 3 teams across Alpha, Bravo, and Charlie categories.

Prize Pool & Categories

Total Prize Pool: PKR 30 million across 3 Cities. 10 million per city. Three competitive categories with 6 clubs each: Alpha (Elite), Bravo (Competitive), and Charlie (Developing).

Player Registration

Open to players aged 16+. Registration requires CNIC, contact info, and short video. Fee: PKR 5,000 (non-refundable). Selected players receive monthly engagement and travel allowances.

Bidding Process

Live auction format with 3 rounds of bidding. Highest bidder wins after 10 seconds of no new bids. Players sign with winning club immediately. 8-12 players per squad with 100% transparent process.
